Beautiful Flowers

Roses are one of the most beloved flowers in the world, often given as a symbol of love and admiration. They come in many colors including red, pink, yellow, and white.

Sunflowers are bright and cheerful flowers that always turn their faces toward the sun. They are native to North America and known for their large golden petals surrounding a dark center.

Lavender is a fragrant purple flower commonly used in aromatherapy and perfumes. It is known for its calming scent and beautiful violet hues that blanket fields across France and England.
